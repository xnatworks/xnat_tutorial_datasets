We want to remove facial features, but be aware that the linear normalization will not precisely match the shape of the brain. In addition, we often want a slightly larger area to allow good intensity normalization. Here is a method to dilate by 8mm

1. Use nimath Euclidean Distance Function to dilate the brain mask by 12mm

niimath mni_icbm152_t1_tal_nlin_asym_55_ext_mask.nii -binv -edt -thr 12 -thr 0 -binv emask12

2. We are just trying to get the mask in the right ballpark, so we can downsample by a factor of 2

python subsamp2.py mni_icbm152_t1_tal_nlin_asym_55_ext.nii MNI152_T1_2mm_ext.nii
python subsamp2.py emask12.nii.gz MNI152_T1_2mm_ext_mask12.nii
